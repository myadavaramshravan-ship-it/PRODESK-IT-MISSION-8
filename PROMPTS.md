## ⭐ Pro Tips

### Getting Better Results

1. **Specific Over General**
   - ❌ Bad: "movie"
   - ✅ Good: "Inception" or "sci-fi"

2. **Use Partial Titles**
   - ✅ Works: "Matrix", "Dark Knight", "Star Wars"

3. **Mix Mood Matcher with Search**
   - Use Mood Matcher for inspiration
   - Then search for specific titles in that genre

4. **Scroll for Variety**
   - Results show multiple movies
   - Infinite scroll reveals more options

5. **Save Favorites Regularly**
   - Click heart icon to bookmark
   - Build your watchlist

### Troubleshooting Searches

**No results found?**
- Check spelling
- Try shorter keywords
- Use simpler terms

**Want different results?**
- Scroll to load more
- Try genre names instead
- Use actor/director names

**Favorite stuck?**
- Refresh the page
- Heart should persist in localStorage
